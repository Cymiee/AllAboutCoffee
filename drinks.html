Classic Coffee Drinks

Most café drinks are combinations of espresso + water + milk (and sometimes foam/chocolate). Ratios vary by café — these are common “baseline” definitions.

Espresso

Concentrated coffee shot. Strong flavor, small volume, crema on top.

Americano

Espresso + hot water. Similar strength to filter coffee but different texture.

Latte

Espresso + lots of steamed milk + a thin layer of foam.

Cappuccino

Espresso + steamed milk + thicker foam (airier texture).

Flat White

Espresso + milk with very thin microfoam. Stronger than a latte.

Mocha

Latte + chocolate (espresso + milk + chocolate).

The “same” drink can vary — cafés differ on ratios and cup sizes.
