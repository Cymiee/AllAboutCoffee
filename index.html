Quick vocabulary

  • Extraction: how much flavor you pull out of the grounds.
  • Ratio: coffee mass to water mass (example: 1:16 for filter coffee).
  • Crema: foam-like layer on espresso from emulsified oils + CO₂.
  • Body: mouthfeel — light/tea-like vs heavy/syrupy.
